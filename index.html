Business model & positioning

One kitchen. Three distribution channels. No compromise on positioning.

🏪 Distribution — 3 channels

  • 5 shops — consistent monthly & bi-weekly orders (B2B, 100+ boxes)
  • 5 churches — sold at a low price so they can bless their congregation, not charge them
  • 1 online store — direct-to-customer, the main brand channel
  • All produced from one kitchen

💻 Online experience

  • Online only — no phone orders
  • Landing page: clear, charismatic, clean
  • Max 4–5 clicks from arrival to paid
  • Confirmation email, auto-forwarded to back office

📱 Channel split

  • 60% TikTok — promote & fish for clients, strategic selling
  • 20% Instagram — character, values, depth
  • 20% Other — open to explore

🚫 Boundaries

  • No festivals
  • No poor positioning
  • Alignment and prosperity only
  • Premium, retail-ready presentation

📦 Open decisions

  • Name for the beef-only box (working title: Bull Box)

  • Confirm wholesale rates (currently 20% off retail)

  • Authentic Jamaican recipe, made locally

  • Faith and values are part of the brand, not a bolt-on
  • Serve the community; don't chase every crowd
